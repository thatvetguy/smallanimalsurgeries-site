About

Small Animal Surgeries, Inc was founded by a veterinarian with experience spanning primary care and emergency medicine. Through years of clinical practice, real-world gaps in the tools available to veterinary professionals and independent contractors became impossible to ignore. That firsthand understanding of daily challenges led to the development of focused, purpose-built software solutions. The mission is simple: turn clinical experience into tools that genuinely help.
